FIRSTEP

Aims

The aim of of the Firstep project was to create my very first freelancing website to sell my developing services to small businesses and sole traders

Process

The project used vanilla OOP PHP and vanilla JavaScript, alongside HTML & CSS. The design comes mainly from the CSS framework Twitter Bootstrap, and was created in the midst of my web development learning.

Outcome

A reasonable first attempt, however, I plan to revisit this website shortly to design a better overall look, with the hopes to increase my client-base.
